Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you require a deposit?

A: Yes, we require a 50% deposit to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due on the day of the event.

Q: How long does editing take?

A: Photography edits usually take 3-5 days. Video edits can take up to 7-14 days depending on the package.

Q: Is transport included?

A: No, transport is calculated separately depending on the distance.
